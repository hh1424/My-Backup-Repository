Having actively participated in college sports, I have experienced the invaluable guidance of a strength coach and a nutritionist who emphasized the importance of incorporating the right macros into our diets for optimal performance. This inspired me to undertake a project that addresses a common struggle many individuals face - creating healthier versions of favorite foods.

Fueled by a desire to share and organize these recipes, I turned to social media, where I found a community eager to explore the same culinary journey. However, the sheer volume of saved recipes on my phone became cumbersome, especially during the cooking process.

To streamline this experience, I envisioned and developed a website connected to a meticulously designed database. This platform allows me, and others alike, to effortlessly search for recipes, follow step-by-step instructions, access ingredient lists, and crucially, manage macronutrient information to align with dietary goals. Through this project, I aim to provide a user-friendly solution for those seeking a healthier lifestyle while enjoying their favorite meals.
